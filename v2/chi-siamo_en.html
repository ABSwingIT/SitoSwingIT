About Us

An Italian SME dedicated to innovation in visualization, XR, and Artificial Intelligence technologies, with a strong focus on research, experimentation, and real-world applications.

We develop complex and innovative solutions for the most advanced research and development projects, effectively meeting the needs of scientific communities.

Software Engineering Italia, founded in 2010, designs and implements computer and telecommunications solutions. Its value lies in the extensive experience accumulated by its human resources.

Software Engineering Italia offers hardware and software solutions for cognitive assessment and rehabilitation, integrating the latest ICT technologies to advance medical research and telemedicine applications.

Swing:It is a leader in Virtual Reality (both classic and highly immersive) and Augmented Reality solutions, commercializing numerous software and hardware products in this field.

The Swing:It team is highly qualified: managers, designers, and developers with over 20 years of professional experience collaborate with a group of bright and dynamic young professionals. The company’s activities are based on collaboration and the continuous exchange of heterogeneous knowledge and skills, both internally and with clients and partners. Swing:It offers clients a new paradigm of design, innovation, modernization, and the creation of new services, leveraging the most modern philosophies and models available in the sector.

ARTES 4.0

Swing:It is a member of the Competence Center Artes 4.0.

ARTES 4.0 is a national Competence Center that supports businesses and public administrations in adopting advanced robotics, artificial intelligence, and digital technologies for Industry 4.0 and 5.0, with a strong focus on manufacturing and services.

  • Provides technological transfer services (test before invest, laboratories, experimental platforms), technical training, and consultancy to integrate intelligent robotics, IoT, AI, and automation into production processes.
  • Coordinates and animates a network of universities, research centers, and over 150 companies to develop and share innovative solutions in sectors such as manufacturing, tourism, healthcare, nautical, fashion, and workplace safety.
  • Promotes projects, calls, and Open Innovation activities (e.g., funding calls, hackathons, demonstrators, thematic events) to facilitate access to funding, accelerate time-to-market, and increase the technological competitiveness of Italian SMEs.

EuroXR

Swing:It is also a member of EuroXR.

EuroXR is the European association dedicated to eXtended Reality (XR), connecting professionals, researchers, and companies in the sector.

  • Creates and manages a professional XR community at the European level, fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking opportunities.
  • Promotes growth and innovation in the XR field through events, exchange of ideas, and building relationships among actors with diverse backgrounds.

  • Strong commitment to applied research and co-funded projects.
  • Integration of XR, AI, and cloud in scalable and maintainable solutions.
