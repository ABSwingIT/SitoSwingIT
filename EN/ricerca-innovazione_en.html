Research and Innovation

Projects in R&S connecting XR, AI, 3D, and cloud infrastructures, in collaboration with national and international partners.

Swing:It has reinvested over 95% of its revenues into Research and development. These investments enable the right path toward Innovation. For this reason, Swing:It favors participation in innovation projects.

Swing:It provides effective solutions to its Clients and Partners aimed at promoting change, offering Project Management, Engineering, Software Design and Development, Training services to ensure success of any Research and Development project. The new paradigms offered by Swing:It guarantee technology transfer to its Partners and enrichment of knowledge and skills for the entire project team.

Since its inception, Software Engineering Italia has taken part, in various roles, in several Research and Development projects in ICT, telemedicine, and Earth Sciences.
