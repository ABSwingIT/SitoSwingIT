Collaborate in CAT-Lab

Page created on 02.12.2024

Applications are open for ICT companies interested in providing consultancy services for the CAT-Lab project, AGRITECH - National Research Centre for Agricultural Technologies, Project Code CN00000022, CUP H93C22000440007, funded under the National Recovery and Resilience Plan, Mission 4 “Education and Research” – Component 2 “From Research to Enterprise” – Investment 1.4, funded by the European Union – NextGenerationEU - CN00000022, CUP H93C22000440007.

Update of 20.12.2024

Consultancy n. 1 € 25,000.00

Specialist consultancy to support the following activities:

  • Definition of requirements for the identification of sensors and advanced IoT devices for accurate monitoring and management of water resources in the field;
  • Identification and analysis of the features of sensors and devices available on the market for the selection of devices for the experimental layout;
  • Identification of requirements for next-generation devices;
  • Design of the installation and data transfer network, including protocols and interfaces.

Expressions of interest must be submitted by 08.01.2025

Update of 01.03.2025

Consultancy n. 2 € 15,000.00

Specialist consultancy to support the following project activities:

  • Implementation and validation of the integrated and interconnected network of sensors and devices.
  • Implementation and testing of the Digital Data Management Platform and its data collection, consumption, visualization, and advanced analysis functionalities, aimed at realizing the Decision Support System;
  • Validation and testing of the LivingLab design and support for its implementation.

Expressions of interest must be submitted by 01.04.2025
