M3 Milano

Since 2004, Swing:It professionals have developed and managed the Tele Video Control Camera (TVCC) platform to support the driverless guidance system and the management of station and train access on the Milan Metro Line 3. Several system upgrades, the last in the period 2010/2011, have been implemented for the client ATM Milan - Line 3.

The Swing:It TVCC solution integrates the ErgoVU Factory Information Portal and the integrated development environment for the visual/Java programming language VIB Laces (now MI Studio), both produced by ErgoTech Systems Inc. The active animations produced by this framework have been embedded into a system portal that enables fully automated interaction between the user and the integrated TVCC system through graphical Human-Machine Interfaces.

This system allows the insertion of customized programs for data acquisition and analysis, process control, report generation, or more generally for anything related to industrial automation, on Windows, Linux, Mac OS platforms, and controllers from various international manufacturers.

Swing:It offers its clients industrial control and automation solutions based on these technologies, including SECS/GEM support, and support for production plants based on assembly lines, suspended transport, etc. Using the same technologies, the company has supported pharmaceutical, automotive, semiconductor, drinking water distribution, and other companies.
